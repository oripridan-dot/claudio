# Claudio — Product Intent

## What Claudio Is

Claudio is a real-time AI musician collaboration platform that analyses acoustic signals and camera input to coach recording artists during tracking, mixing, and mastering.

It sees what a seasoned engineer sees, hears what a seasoned engineer hears, and delivers that knowledge in plain language — as it happens.

## Who It Serves

Solo musicians, home-studio recordists, and small-band producers who operate without a dedicated recording engineer. Claudio is the engineer they don't have.

## Non-Negotiable Features

| Feature | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Instrument & model identification | Know exactly what is being played — family, pickup type, make/model — to give accurate advice |
| Holographic binaural rendering | 192 kHz HRTF-based spatial audio with sub-1.5 ms head-tracking latency |
| Room acoustic scanning | RT60, room modes, flutter echo, early reflections — actionable treatment plans |
| Gesture-controlled mixing | Camera-driven fader, pan, and effect control via hand and head gestures |
| Progressive roadmap | Guide users through setup → tracking → mixing → mastering → release |
| Mentor system | Real coaching advice from the perspectives of legendary engineers |
| Semantic metering | Pocket Radar, frequency collision maps, performance coaching — not just dBFS |
| Zero-latency observation | AI analysis runs on the observation path; the live audio path is never touched |

## What Claudio Is Not

- A DAW. Claudio does not record, edit, or play back audio. It operates alongside the user's DAW.
- A plugin. Claudio is a standalone AI platform that communicates via WebSocket with its own UI.
- A replacement for human ears. Claudio is a coach — the musician makes the final call.
